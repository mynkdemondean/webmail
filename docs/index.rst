How to Contact Roadrunner Customer Support
------------------------------------------

Roadrunner support can be reached in multiple ways. Here’s how to get help quickly:

1. **Call Roadrunner Customer Service**
   
   - **Official Phone Number (U.S.): 1-800-833-7676**  
   - Steps:
     1. Dial the number from your phone.  
     2. Follow the automated menu and choose your issue category (Email, Internet, Billing).  
     3. Wait for a representative and explain your problem clearly.  
     4. Provide account details if required.  

2. **Use Online Support**
   
   - Visit: `https://www.spectrum.net/support/`  
   - Steps:
     1. Select your issue type: Email, Internet, Billing, or Technical Support.  
     2. Browse FAQs or troubleshooting guides for immediate help.  
     3. If the problem persists, use the **Live Chat** feature to speak with a support agent.  

3. **Email Support Through Spectrum Account**
   
   - Steps:
     1. Log in at `https://www.spectrum.net/`.  
     2. Go to **Support > Contact Us**.  
     3. Choose **Email Support** and describe your issue.  
     4. Wait for a response within 24–48 hours.  

4. **Social Media Assistance**
   
   - Roadrunner/Spectrum is active on Twitter and Facebook:  
     - Twitter: [@Ask_Spectrum](https://twitter.com/Ask_Spectrum)  
     - Facebook: [Spectrum Support](https://www.facebook.com/Spectrum)

Step-by-Step Guide to Solve Common Roadrunner Problems
-------------------------------------------------------

**1. Roadrunner Email Login Issues**  

- Visit: `https://webmail.rr.com/`  
- Enter your full email and password.  
- If login fails:  
  1. Click **Forgot Password?**  
  2. Follow the verification steps to reset your password.  
  3. Retry logging in with the new password.

**2. Emails Not Sending or Receiving**  

- Check your internet connection.  
- Verify server settings:  
  - Incoming: IMAP – `imap.services.rr.com` / POP3 – `pop.services.rr.com`  
  - Outgoing: SMTP – `smtp.services.rr.com`  
- Make sure your mailbox is not full.  
- Contact support if the problem continues.

**3. Slow or Unstable Internet**  

- Restart your modem and router.  
- Check all cable connections.  
- Run an internet speed test.  
- If issues persist, call **1-800-833-7676** for technical support.

**4. Billing or Account Issues**  

- Log in to your Spectrum account.  
- Navigate to **Billing & Payments**.  
- Review invoices and charges.  
- Contact support for disputed charges or subscription changes.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
---------------------------------

**Q1: How long does it take to get a response from Roadrunner support?**  
A: Phone and live chat support are immediate. Email responses usually take 24–48 hours.

**Q2: Can I reset my Roadrunner email password without calling support?**  
A: Yes, use the **Forgot Password** option on `https://webmail.rr.com/`.

**Q3: Does Roadrunner support international customers?**  
A: Roadrunner (Spectrum) primarily supports U.S. customers. International users may need alternative contact methods.

**Q4: What if my email is hacked or compromised?**  
A: Contact support immediately and follow the security recovery steps provided by Roadrunner/Spectrum.
